Redstonic

This is the download page for Raizunne's Redstonic mod.

Redstonic Drills are the main focus of this mod. With these, you can modify your drills however you want. Change its head, body and the energy that the drill has and aud augments to increase the drill capabilities!

Redstonic Drills are made up from 3 basic components.

  • Drill Head
  • Drill Body
  • Flux Capacitor from the mod Thermal Expansion

    • With these, you will need to join them together in the Drill Modifier. You can see a picture of the Drill Modifier's GUI in the picture secion below.

    For more information on the mod, drill heads or bodies, you can consult the mod's guide inside of the game, called the Redstonic Manual.

    You are more than welcome to add this mod to your modpack and distribute it. You are NOT allowed to GAIN money from a modpack with this mod.
