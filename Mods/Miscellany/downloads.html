Miscellany

This is the download page for Raizunne's Miscellany mod.

Miscellany adds several different machines and tools that can make your experience in Minecraft easier, such as the Food Packager, Packager, Knowledge Gem, new brewing system, new potions and the Sacred Chalice!

Discover better brewing.

Experience adding potions, flying potions and more.

Spectacular new items.

Experience storing, unlimited water, explosions.

Compression is the new way to go.

Compress up to 576 items into a single box.

With in-game manual.

Specially coded to be intuitive and fast to learn.
