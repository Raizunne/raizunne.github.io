Redstonic Drill - Redstonic

Redstonic Drill is the base of the mod Redstonic. This is a mining drill that uses RF power to mine! The drill is made up of 3 basic components: Drill Head, Drill Body and Drill Battery. The drill is fully customizable and depending on the type of body you can have up to 3 drill augments.
